Seoul Trip 2022

7 Aug 2022 to 14 Aug 2022

During my Year 1 Summer break, I took a trip to Seoul, South Korea. I went with my parents and one of my university friend. Here was what happened during our eventful journey!

Personally, I enjoy visiting South Korea as I am an avid fan of K-Pop and K-Drama. With my interest, I have also self-learnt the basics of the Korean language, which I later found to be especially useful in my trip!

Day 1: Arrival, Myeongdong, Dongdaemun

On Day 1, we arrived in Seoul around 7am. After settling down in the hotel, we hit the streets of Myeongdong, and visited a few malls in Dongdaemun. A lot of the shops in Myeongdong unfortunately closed due to COVID, but the vibe was still good!

    Food:
  • Kyoja @ Myeongdong
  • Doenjang Jjigae @ Doota Mall
    • Day 2: Express Bus Terminal, IFC Mall

    The focus of the next day was shopping!! We went to Express Bus Terminal, which we knew had the best prices for clothing. Based on our itinerary, we were supposed to visit the Han River. However, this was coincidentally also the day that Han River got flooded with the extreme rain in Seoul. As such, we settled on going to a high-end mall in Yeouido, IFC mall.

      Food:
  • Paik's Coffee and Churros @ Express Bus Terminal
  • Mama Doenjang @ IFC Mall
    • Day 3: Lotte World, Lotte Mall

    When we go to Seoul, we definitely have to visit Lotte World! The last time I visited Lotte World, I enjoyed myself a lot. The outdoor rides were especially thrilling. Unfortunately, due to the rain, most of the outdoor rides were closed :( Nonetheless, we still had a great time in the amusement park. Thereafter, we went to Lotte Mart for grocery shopping!

      Food:
  • Hotdog @ Lotte World
  • Naengmyeong @ Seoul Station
    • Day 4: Jongro, Ikseondong, Tosokchon, Gwangjang Market

    Unexpectedly, this was actually my favourite day! We decided to take a trip down to the smaller neighbourhoods in Seoul, and found that Jongro (the office area) and Ikseondong had much to offer! Not only were there a lot of good food places, there was also a lot to see, and we felt that we had a glimpse into the daily lives of Korean people :)

      Food:
  • Cheese Tart @ Jongro
  • Doenjang Jjigae @ Jongro
  • Odeng & Mandu @ Gwangjang Market
  • Bread & Cake @ Ikseondong
